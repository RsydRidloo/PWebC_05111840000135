Food



Becek Menthok

This particular food looks like curry. Has a very large sauce with a thick texture. Menthok meat is also one of Tuban's mainstays of food. This food is made from the basic ingredients of menthok meat which is seasoned with a blend of spices the spices used are pepper, cumin, shallots, garlic, mesoyi, bay leaves, kaffir lime leaves, lemongrass, tomatoes, coconut milk, and a sprinkling of chopped green onions.

Dumbek

This one snack is a typical food that may not be known by many young people today. The reason is none other than because this food is rarely found in Tuban. Dumbek is a soft textured food wrapped in palm leaves. This snack is oval shaped like a trumpet and has a fragrant smell. It is said that the maker said this snack was a snack for the guardians.

Kare Rajungan

The geography of Tuban Regency which is located on the coast of the North Coast makes this regency has a lot of food made from marine animals. The sea animal referred to is crab crabs. The crab is a marine animal that is similar to crab, in Tuban the crab is cooked with curry sauce. Tuban specialty food this one is famous for its spicy taste, for those of you who like to eat spicy, please try this crab curry, this typical tuban food. The basic crab is processed with special spices, the basic spices used are bay leaves, lemongrass stalk, laos, coconut milk, tamarind water, all cooked and the crabs are added until the spices are absorbed. Then the crab curry is ready to be served.

Garang Asem

Garang Asem itself is processed food made from free-range chicken which is given coconut milk sauce. Garang Asem is wrapped in banana leaves when cooked. Besides that, Garang Asem also has a very sour and spicy taste that is very integrated. Garang Asem which is located in Tuban City has its own characteristics when compared to Garang Asem in general. The very distinctive feature of this typical garang-sour Tuban is the clear, generous, and fresh sauce.
