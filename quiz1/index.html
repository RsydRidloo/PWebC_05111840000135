Hometown

Tuban Regency is a regency in East Java which is located on the North Coast of East Java. This district with a population of around 1.2 million people consists of 20 sub-districts and thousands of cities in Tuban District. Tuban Regency has a strategic location, which is on the border of East Java and Central Java Provinces, which is crossed by Daendels National Road on the North Coast. Tuban Regency is directly adjacent to Rembang to the west, Lamongan to the east, and Bojonegoro to the south. The administrative center of Tuban Regency is located 100 km northwest of Surabaya, the capital of East Java Province and 210 km east of Semarang, the capital of Central Java Province. Therefore, in ancient times Tuban was used as the main port of the Majapahit Kingdom and became one of the centers for the spread of Islam by the Walisongo.

Tuban Regency is on the northern coast line and in the North Cretaceous mountain range. The North Limestone Mountains in Tuban stretch from Jatirogo District to Widang District, and from Merakurak District to Soko District. Meanwhile, the sea area stretches between 5 districts, namely Bancar District, Tambakboyo District, Jenu District, Tuban District and Palang District. Tuban Regency is at the northern end and western part of East Java, which is directly on the border of East Java and Central Java or between Tuban and Rembang Regencies. Tuban has the lowest point, which is 0 m asl which is on the Pantura Line and the highest point is 500 m which is located in the Grabagan District. Tuban is also traversed by the Bengawan Solo River which flows from Solo to Gresik.

Food



Becek Menthok

This particular food looks like curry. Has a very large sauce with a thick texture. Menthok meat is also one of Tuban's mainstays of food. This food is made from the basic ingredients of menthok meat which is seasoned with a blend of spices the spices used are pepper, cumin, shallots, garlic, mesoyi, bay leaves, kaffir lime leaves, lemongrass, tomatoes, coconut milk, and a sprinkling of chopped green onions.

Dumbek

This one snack is a typical food that may not be known by many young people today. The reason is none other than because this food is rarely found in Tuban. Dumbek is a soft textured food wrapped in palm leaves. This snack is oval shaped like a trumpet and has a fragrant smell. It is said that the maker said this snack was a snack for the guardians.

Kare Rajungan

The geography of Tuban Regency which is located on the coast of the North Coast makes this regency has a lot of food made from marine animals. The sea animal referred to is crab crabs. The crab is a marine animal that is similar to crab, in Tuban the crab is cooked with curry sauce. Tuban specialty food this one is famous for its spicy taste, for those of you who like to eat spicy, please try this crab curry, this typical tuban food. The basic crab is processed with special spices, the basic spices used are bay leaves, lemongrass stalk, laos, coconut milk, tamarind water, all cooked and the crabs are added until the spices are absorbed. Then the crab curry is ready to be served.

Garang Asem

Garang Asem itself is processed food made from free-range chicken which is given coconut milk sauce. Garang Asem is wrapped in banana leaves when cooked. Besides that, Garang Asem also has a very sour and spicy taste that is very integrated. Garang Asem which is located in Tuban City has its own characteristics when compared to Garang Asem in general. The very distinctive feature of this typical garang-sour Tuban is the clear, generous, and fresh sauce.

Tourist

Nglirip Waterfall

Nglirip Waterfall is located in Mulyo Agung Village, precisely in Jojogan Hamlet, Singgahan District. The distance between these villages from downtown Tuban is about 37 km from the Southwest. Its location, which is not too far from the city center of Tuban, is also very easy to reach by using private vehicles or by using public transportation.

Coconut Beach

The condition of this waterfall is still very natural and the trees that grow around it are very thick and big. This tourist spot is named coconut beach because indeed in this tourist location, various types of coconut trees with various shapes and sizes thrive.

In the past, visitors were only served with coconut trees and wide white sand, but for now visitors can find a bridge deck on a stage. The coolness of the coconut beach is second to none, because some coconut trees are quite dense and shady. This is a strong reason why this beach in Tuban is always suitable as a port to rest a tired mind due to many work activities.

Ngerong Cave

So far, Tuban Regency is known as Seribu Goa. This term is certainly not just nonsense, in this district there are many caves that are very old and sometimes even sacred. One of the most famous caves in Tuban is Goa Ngerong which is located in Rengel District, Tuban Regency, East Java. Goa Ngerong is famous for its beauty and the existence of springs that come out of the cave. In this cave, there are also tawes and turtles, which are one of the main attractions that until now still have uncovered myths. Apart from fish and turtles, during the day visitors can enjoy the thousands of bats hanging from the roof of the cave. Apart from its beautiful nature, thousands of living things in Goa add to the uniqueness of Goa Ngerong.
